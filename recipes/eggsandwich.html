Egg Sandwich

Sausage, Egg, and Cheese Sandwich

The eggsandwich has been a breakfast icon since the 19th century. It's found in almost every breakfast shop and can be made in just about every home. America can be said to have a taste for eggs with certain food chains cooking millions of eggs a year

One of the main reasons eggsandwich's are so loved lies in there ingredients, taking only three things we can find in every home wich are eggs, bread, and cheese. Just these three things can make a satisfying breakfast but it doesn't need to end cheese. Some ingredients others may add on top could be bacon, ham, and sausage. When it comes to the egg Sandwich it really is up to you how it tastes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Stove Top:You will want to start by heating up your pan on the cook top. Most middle settings on cook tops are perfect for eggs.
  2. Bread:While you wait for the pan to heat up it is a good idea to start the process of toasting the bread of your choice.
  3. The Egg:Now that your pan has heated to a good temp, you want to butter it up then crack your egg in.
  4. Cooking:Breaking the yolk is up to the cook in how they want the egg to come out. You will want to cook the egg for about 2-3 minutes before you flip it. You will only need to flip once.
  5. The Flip:Once you get to this point the process is nearly done. When there is only a minute or two left on the timer where gonna want to put our cheese and toppings of chose.
  6. Finishing Up:Around this time your bread should be done toasting. Take it out and butter both sides with light butter, making sure to evenly spread it. After scrape your egg sandwich off the pand and slide it onto the bottom bread slice.
  7. Eat:If you have followed all the steps you should have a great, tasteful breakfast!
